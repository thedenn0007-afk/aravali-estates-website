Who We Are

Aravali Estates — Redefining Bangalore Luxury

Founded in 2006 by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Rajesh Nair, Aravali Estates began with a single mission: to build homes that Bangaloreans would be proud to live in. What started as a boutique developer with one project in Koramangala has grown into one of the city's most respected real estate brands.

Today, we operate across 8 micro-markets in Bangalore — from the tech corridors of Whitefield and Sarjapur to the premium neighbourhoods of Hebbal and Indiranagar. Every project we deliver reflects our core philosophy: quality without compromise, delivered on time, every time.

We are RERA registered, ISO 9001:2015 certified, and proud recipients of the CREDAI Excellence Award for four consecutive years.
