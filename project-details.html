Project Overview

Green Valley Villas is Aravali Estates' most prestigious villa community — a testament to thoughtful design and meticulous craftsmanship. Nestled across 22 acres of verdant landscape along Sarjapur Road, each villa is an architectural masterpiece with double-height living areas, Italian marble flooring, and private garden spaces that blur the line between indoor and outdoor living.

The project features only 42 exclusive units, ensuring an intimate community feel with maximum privacy. Each home comes pre-installed with a Schneider smart home system, designer modular kitchen, and premium sanitary fittings from Kohler and Duravit.
